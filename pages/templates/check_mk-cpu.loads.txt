===== check_mk-cpu.loads =====

==== Template Description ====

^ Author ^
| Romuald FRONTEAU |

This Template can be used with [[http://mathias-kettner.de/check_mk.html|check_mk]] written by Mattias Kettner. I modify PNP Template of Mattias to add : 

  * Black background
  * burning flame effect for the 5 minutes curve and a projection line.
  * A LINE2 for the 15 minutes curve
